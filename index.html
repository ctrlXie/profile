My Favorite Guitars

Fender American Special Strat

Easily recognizable by its large headstock, the fender american special stratocaster is in the affordable range for those who want the larger headstock

Gibson Les Paul SG

Popularized by iconic AC/DC front-man, Angus Young, the Gibson SG puts out enough crunch to irritate your neighbors. Its cutaway body makes it ideal for solos, giving rockers a wide range of fretboard to work with.

Fender '72 Deluxe Telecaster

One of my favorite looking guitars. The '72 deluxe comes with the fatter headstock, which I love. Hardware is styled antique, to give it that classic '72 look.
